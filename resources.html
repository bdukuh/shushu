Rental and Special Events

Where are the best parties – or corporate events, family reunions, fundraisers, receptions, weddings, or other special occasions where the right location is crucial? The Leisure Services Department offers a wide variety of venues for many types of events, including art galleries, gyms, multi-purpose rooms, picnic pavilions, a professional theater, and more. Whether you are planning a small meeting, lavish gala, or one of life’s many celebrations, attendees will appreciate the convenient indoor and outdoor locations and inviting environment.
